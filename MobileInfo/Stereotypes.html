Breaking the Stereo-Types .

Stereo-Types are those pre-present information in the brain that are formed from personal experience ,seeing others opinion or following some one blindly.For Example small kids follow actions of their elders with out knowing either it is right or wrong unless they understand what they're doing .
Even many of us follow others opinion in buying stuff or devices whereas we should be studying on our own to know much better on what we are spending.
Well the first thing i want to do is to remove all the stereotypes that are formed in you by any other means.
At first, Let me make this clear that no smartphone is good or bad according to their Branding or manufacturer.
Nor it is like that "The Higher you pay the better you get"..
Its just simply picking what you require ,which one suites your budget and Which is worthy to the price paying for it.
So, the main question is If one manufacturer with same product specification is selling its device for lets say 70,000rs and the other for 30,000rs...then why this high price difference Right?
The answer to this is every manufacturer has to come up with the expenditure of thier emloyees ,raw material,advertising,producing cost,as well as profit etcetra......
So, it varies from manufacturer to manufacturer according to their expenses. The good thing is if the product specification is same then the performance and every thing would never change....
Then why pay more?? ...
So, No branding is necessary for a smartphone being good.
