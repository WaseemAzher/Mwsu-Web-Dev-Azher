Basic Over view of Smartphone Manufacturers .

Smartphones are small hand held electronic devices with mobile operating and an integrated mobile broadband cellular network connection for voice, SMS, and Internet data communication; most if not all smartphones also support Wi-Fi
These devices are a product of combination of small and large components like mainly display,motherboard,soc,camera,ram,rom...etc.
Each component has its own functionality that serves the users
All these small components joins together and form a Device.
In this emerging technology many of the manufacturer are evolving their smartphone technology at higher cost every time whereas there are also some small manufacturers that are offering same features at a low cost...
So different smartphones have different set of components wiht different features and specification which makes an individual confusing in selecting a device from around billions of devices from thousands of manufacturers

The same case is with IPhone 7+ and Iphone 8+...
Another Huge emerging company, Samsung does the same thing with the Samsung S8 and S8+by relaunching it In the name of S9 and S9+ .The innovation here is the Manual aperture set in camera which is new technology in smartphones and often seen in DSLR cameras ,But compared to the previous company this is quite acceptable ..
